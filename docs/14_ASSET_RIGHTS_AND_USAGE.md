# Asset Rights and Usage

## Production-safe assets

- Designs Direct logo supplied by the user.
- Original SVGs created for this project under `public/ui`.
- Original everyday placeholder SVGs under `public/placeholders/everyday`.
- Exact cleaned product photographs created from Designs Direct's own source photos after review.
- Original Web Audio cues in `lib/sound/synth.ts`.

## Reference-only assets

The images under `private/reference-only` include television stills, logos, and wiki-derived references supplied by the user. They are included to communicate visual direction to the developer, not as permission to deploy, trace, or redistribute them.

Use them to study general ideas such as:

- color blocking;
- scenic framing;
- bulb placement;
- mechanical number displays;
- price-tag silhouettes;
- product pedestal composition.

Create original CSS, SVG, typography, motion, and sound for the production app.
